SheComply-An-AI-Compliance-Guidance-System
We propose an AI-Powered Compliance Guidance System for Women Entrepreneurs. The system: 1)Provides automated, step-by-step compliance assistance 2)Simplifies taxation and licensing requirements 3)Offers multilingual support 4)Generates personalized compliance checklists 5)Calculates a dynamic compliance readiness score

Key Capabilities: 📌 Taxation guidance (GST, income tax basics) 📄 License and registration walkthrough 📝 Legal document checklist generator 📊 Compliance scoring dashboard 🤖 Conversational AI assistant for queries The platform transforms complex regulations into simple, actionable guidance.
